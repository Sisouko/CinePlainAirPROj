Frequently Asked Questions
What is CinemaFes and how does it work? +
CinemaFes is a subscription-based streaming platform that lets you watch movies, series, documentaries, and exclusive originals on any connected device — no ads, no contracts, just great content.
How much does a subscription cost? +
Plans start from $8.99/month. Choose from Basic, Standard, or Premium depending on screens and video quality. All plans include unlimited access with no hidden fees.
On which devices can I watch CinemaFes? +
Watch on Smart TVs, phones, tablets, laptops, gaming consoles, and streaming sticks. Your account works on up to 4 devices simultaneously on the Premium plan.
Can I download content for offline viewing? +
Yes! Download your favorite movies and series to watch offline on mobile and tablet devices. Downloads are available on Standard and Premium plans.
Is there a free trial available? +
New members get a 30-day free trial with full access to all content. Cancel anytime before the trial ends and you won't be charged.
How do I cancel my subscription? +
Cancel anytime from your account settings — no fees, no hoops to jump through. If you cancel, you'll still have access until the end of your billing period.
